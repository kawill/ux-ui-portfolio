Ameritest Survey Redesign

Survey Redesign for Mobile Optimization

Summary

I shipped a co–led redesign of our main survey for mobile optimization at a marketing research consultancy:

  • Chose darker theme to allow tested visual content to stand out and reduce eye strain
  • Customized survey theme through CSS/Less & JavaScript/jQuery updating
  • Updated automatically generated survey code
  • Presented standardization of survey questions including a shift to mobile– & user–friendly interactive & dynamic questions to stakeholders
  • Performed cross-platform compatibility testing

Result: This redesign gave us the opportunity to expand our audience and expand on and create new offerings such as copy testing in mock social media environments.
